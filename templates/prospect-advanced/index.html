The data gap

Most teams know who attended. Very few know what it was worth.

An "attended: yes" field in your CRM is a starting point, not an answer. The difference between an attendee record and an event-qualified lead record is what happened during the session.

The most revenue-attributable events programs sync a score, not a boolean. Every interaction — questions asked, polls answered, CTA clicks, replay views — is a signal. Together they produce a lead quality signal your sales team can actually act on.

The integration is where most of this lives or dies. If the field mapping is shallow, the data never gets used.

What a good CRM sync looks like

✓ Attendance duration (not just "attended")
✓ Questions asked in Q&A (intent signal)
✓ Poll responses (interest profiling)
✓ CTA clicks (action signal)
✓ Replay views + watch depth
→ Composite engagement score → lead routing
